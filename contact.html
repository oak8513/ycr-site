Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment to visit your York, PA shop?

No — walk-ins are welcome any time during business hours. We're open Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 5 PM, and closed Saturday and Sunday. Just bring your computer to 2069 Carlisle Rd in York and we'll check it in at the counter.

How much does it cost to get my computer looked at?

Our diagnostic fee is $39.99 for standard laptops and desktops, or $99 for gaming PCs. That's the only charge before you decide whether to repair. After the diagnostic, we'll quote the repair separately so you can decide if it's worth fixing.

How long will my repair take?

Most diagnostics are done same-day or next business day. Repair time depends on what's wrong — software cleanups and tune-ups are usually 24–48 hours, while parts-dependent repairs (replacement screens, batteries, drives) may take a few business days while parts ship to our York shop. We'll give you a realistic timeline before you approve the work.

What kinds of computers do you repair?

We work on Windows laptops, desktop PCs, gaming rigs, and custom builds from major brands like HP, Dell, Lenovo, ASUS, Acer, MSI, Razer, Samsung and LG. We do not service Apple / Mac computers, phones, tablets, or Microsoft Surface devices.

Will my personal files and photos be safe?

Yes. We treat your data with strict confidentiality and only access what's required to diagnose and fix the problem. If a repair could put data at risk — like a failing hard drive — we'll explain it and recommend a backup plan before we touch anything.

Where exactly are you located?

We're at 2069 Carlisle Rd, York, PA 17408, with easy free parking and ground-floor access. We're a quick drive from downtown York, Shiloh, West Manchester, Spring Garden Township, and most other York County neighborhoods.

Can I just call instead of stopping by?

Absolutely. Call or text us at 717-739-9675 during business hours and we'll usually pick up. You can also email help@yorkcomputerrepair.com and we'll respond within one business day.
