SSD & RAM Upgrades

If your computer is slow but otherwise working fine, it might just need an upgrade rather than a replacement. Switching from an old hard drive to an SSD is the single biggest performance improvement you can make — boot times can drop dramatically — often under a minute. Adding RAM stops the sluggishness when you have too many tabs open.

  • ✓ SSD installation (SATA & NVMe)
  • ✓ Data migration from old drive
  • ✓ RAM upgrade (4GB → 8GB → 16GB+)
  • ✓ Compatibility check before purchase
  • ✓ Windows reinstall & setup included
  • ✓ Before & after speed benchmarks

Pro tip: Most laptops 3–6 years old are great candidates for an SSD upgrade. It's often cheaper than buying a new computer and gives you 3+ more years of fast performance.
