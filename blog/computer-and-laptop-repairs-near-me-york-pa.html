Common Laptop and Desktop Problems We Fix Daily

Most computer problems fall into a few categories. Here's what we see every week at our York shop:

Screen issues: Cracked laptop screens, flickering displays, or black screens that won't light up. Screen replacement usually takes a day, and we stock parts for HP, Dell, Lenovo, ASUS, Acer, MSI, Razer, Samsung, LG, and custom builds.

Slow performance: Your computer takes ten minutes to boot up, programs freeze, or everything runs like molasses. Often this is a dying hard drive, maxed-out RAM, or malware. Upgrading to an SSD and adding RAM can make a five-year-old laptop feel brand new.

Virus and malware infections: Pop-ups, ransomware warnings, browsers redirecting to sketchy sites. We remove the infection and explain how it got there so you don't get hit again.

Won't turn on: Dead power supply, failed motherboard, or just a loose cable. We diagnose the cause and tell you if repair makes sense or if it's time for a new machine.

Data recovery: Deleted files, corrupted drives, or a laptop that died before you backed up your photos. We recover what's salvageable and help you set up automatic backups going forward.

Gaming PC Repairs and Custom Builds for York Gamers

Gaming PCs need specialized knowledge. A custom rig with liquid cooling, overclocked components, and RGB lighting isn't the same as a Dell office laptop.

We troubleshoot gaming PC issues like thermal throttling, GPU failures, boot loops, and driver conflicts. We also build custom gaming PCs from scratch if you want to upgrade or start fresh. Tell us your budget and what games you play, and we'll spec out a build that makes sense.

Gaming PCs take longer to diagnose because we stress-test the GPU, check thermals under load, and verify your overclock is stable—so expect the diagnostic to take more time than a standard laptop.
