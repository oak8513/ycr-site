Frequently Asked Questions

How much does laptop screen replacement cost in York, PA? +

Cost varies by laptop model and screen type — touch screens and higher-resolution panels cost more than standard displays. We quote you exactly after inspecting the laptop, before any work begins.

How long does a screen replacement take? +

If we have your screen in stock, same-day or next-day replacement is common. If we need to order the exact panel for your model, it typically takes 2–4 business days.

Can you replace a touch screen laptop display? +

Yes. Touch screen replacements are more involved and the parts cost more, but we handle them regularly. Bring it in and we'll confirm the correct replacement panel for your exact model.

My screen is cracked but the laptop works fine otherwise — is it worth fixing? +

Usually yes. If the rest of the laptop runs well, a screen replacement gives you years more use at a fraction of the cost of a new laptop. We'll tell you honestly if the repair doesn't make financial sense.

Do you offer a warranty on laptop screen replacements? +

Yes. Replacement screens are covered under our standard repair warranty. If the new panel develops a defect after installation — such as dead pixels or backlight failure unrelated to physical damage — bring it back and we'll make it right at no additional charge.

Will I lose my files or programs when you replace the screen? +

No. A laptop screen replacement is a hardware-only repair — your hard drive, files, applications, and Windows installation are untouched. Your laptop boots up exactly as it was when you brought it in. If you're worried about your data for any reason, we can also discuss a backup before the repair starts.

Should I bring my laptop charger when I drop it off? +

Yes — please bring the charger. We need to power the laptop on during diagnosis and again to test the new screen before you pick it up. If you've lost the original charger, let us know your laptop model and we can usually pair it with one we have on hand.
