Frequently Asked Questions

How much does data recovery cost in York, PA? +

Cost depends on the type and severity of failure. Logical recovery (deleted files, corruption) is typically $99–$199. Mechanical hard drive recovery is more complex and priced after assessment. We always quote upfront.

Can you recover data from a drive that won't spin up? +

Possibly, yes. Bring it in for an assessment. Some non-spinning drives have failed PCB boards that can be repaired or swapped. Severely mechanically damaged drives may require clean-room recovery services we can refer you to.

Can you recover files I accidentally deleted? +

Often yes, especially if you haven't written much new data to the drive since deleting the files. The sooner you stop using the drive and bring it in, the better the recovery odds.

Do you recover data from SSDs? +

Yes, we perform SSD and NVMe data recovery. SSD recovery is more complex than traditional hard drives due to TRIM and wear-leveling, but many logical failures are recoverable.
