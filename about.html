About York Computer Repair

A local repair shop built on honesty, expertise, and care for the York community.

Who We Are

York Computer Repair is a locally owned and operated computer repair shop serving York County, Pennsylvania. We're part of York Computer — a full-service technology company covering everything from walk-in repairs to managed IT services for businesses.

The repair side of our business — the one you're looking at right now — is all about fixing your device as quickly and affordably as possible. No upselling, no unnecessary parts, no confusing jargon. We tell you what's wrong, what it costs, and whether it's worth fixing. Simple as that.

We're located at 2069 Carlisle Rd in York, easy to find and easy to get to. Walk-ins are always welcome — no appointment needed for a free diagnostic.

Our Promise to York County

We started York Computer Repair because we kept seeing people get ripped off or talked into buying a new computer when their old one just needed a simple fix. That's not right.

Our promise is simple: we'll always tell you the truth. If your laptop is worth fixing, we'll fix it. If buying a new one makes more financial sense, we'll tell you that too — even if it means losing the job.

That's how you earn trust in a community. And York has been good to us, so we plan to keep earning it.
