How a Laptop Repair Works at Our Shop

The process is simple and there are no surprises. You walk in (or call ahead — either is fine), we take a quick look and ask a few questions about what the laptop is doing. We accept the unit, run a hands-on diagnostic, and call or message you with the actual problem and what it would cost to fix. You decide whether to proceed — we don't start any paid work without your okay.

Our diagnostic fee is a flat $39.99 for standard laptops and $99 for gaming laptops (the higher rate reflects the extra time required to test high-end CPUs, discrete GPUs, and custom cooling). The diagnostic fee applies toward the repair if you choose to move forward. We do not offer "free diagnostics" — that usually means hidden costs somewhere else, and we'd rather just be straight with you about pricing up front.

Most common laptop repairs are finished in 1–3 business days. Screen replacements and parts orders for less-common models can take longer, but we always give you a realistic timeline before we start. If a repair will need a special part shipped from out of state, you'll know that on day one — not a week in.
